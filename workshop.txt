description=[h2]Sandbox Options[/h2]
description=- Define the start and end days for each type of events.
description=- New automatic player speech during events.
description=- New effect system for events (Fear, Sickness, Burn/Wound).
description=- Sound events now have more depth and features.
description=- Deaf Zombies, they will not hear the sound events.
description=- Fine tune the cooldown between the next sound event.
description=- Disable sound packs without restarting the server.
description=
description=[h2]3 sound packs included[/h2]
description=[b]Make sure to at least have one pack enabled.[/b]
description=
description=[b]Disasters[/b]
description=- Contains 2 Earthquake sounds.
description=- Earth Quake will now make you stressed and panicked unless you are brave enough.
description=
description=[b]Sirens[/b]
description=- Contains 3 Raid Siren sounds.
description=- Raid Sirens will now make you stressed and panicked unless you are brave enough.
description=
description=[b]Nukes[/b]
description=- Contains 3 Nuke Explosion sounds.
description=- Nuclear explosion can now burn your bodyparts.
description=- Nuclear explosion radiation will now make you sick.
description=- Use the proper protection to be safe from the negative effects.
description=- Nuclear explosion will now make you stressed and panicked unless you are brave enough.
description=- Hide inside a building or a vehicle to stay safe.
description=
description=[i]Activate all the packs that you want. You can disabled them in the Sandbox Options as you need.[/i]
